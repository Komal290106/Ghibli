Magical Movies

Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle

A young woman cursed with old age finds refuge in Howl's magical moving castle. A tale of love, war, and self-acceptance.

Released: 2004

Fun fact: The castle design was inspired by various European architectures!

My Neighbor Totoro

My Neighbor Totoro

Two sisters discover magical forest creatures when they move to the countryside with their father.

Released: 1988

Fun fact: Totoro became Studio Ghibli's mascot and symbol of childhood wonder!

Spirited Away

Spirited Away

A young girl works in a mystical bathhouse to save her parents from a curse.

Released: 2001

Fun fact: First anime to win an Academy Award for Best Animated Feature!

Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki's Delivery Service

A young witch starts her own delivery service while finding her place in the world.

Released: 1989

Fun fact: The city was inspired by Stockholm and other European cities!

Ponyo

Ponyo

A magical fish girl befriends a young boy in this oceanic adventure of friendship.

Released: 2008

Fun fact: Every frame was hand-drawn - over 170,000 individual images!

Castle in the Sky

Castle in the Sky

A young girl and boy search for a legendary floating castle and its ancient secrets.

Released: 1986

Fun fact: This was Studio Ghibli's very first film production!

The Heart Behind the Magic

Hayao Miyazaki - the visionary storyteller whose films remind us that courage, kindness, and wonder live in the smallest moments. His worlds breathe with hand-drawn sincerity, and every frame hums with life.

From My Neighbor Totoro to The Wind Rises, Miyazaki continues to show that animation is not just art - it's emotion in motion, a testament to the beauty found in everyday magic.
